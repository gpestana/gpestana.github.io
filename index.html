Hi there! I'm Gonçalo, glad that you are around.

I'm a software engineer at F-Secure currently working in the Rapid Detection Service backend team. I delve into all things microservices, Openstack, docker, DCOS, Elasticsearch, security and many others in a daily basis. Never without a warm mug of coffee by my side.

I graduated from Tecnico Lisboa in Network communication engineering and I'm currently finishing my Software engineering master's at Aalto University.

I've been working as a software consultant for over 3 years now, playing around with Node.js, Python, data analysis, devops and web stuff. Along the way I've collaborated with some cool companies like CERN, Fivestars, Catchbox and Aalto.

I started North Impact. At North Impact we aim at solving the most challenging social problems through beautifully crafted software and hardware. Just because I love a good challenge, that's why..

I was once a hockey player and did win some stuff: a couple of European leagues and was top2 in the World Championship in Chile. Now I put all my energies into climbing and coding (sometimes simultaneously). I'm also an avid traveller and I enjoy a good smoothie!
